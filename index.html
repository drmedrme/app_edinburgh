Draft slots for travel, festival shows, meals, and fallback options. Status and notes are saved in this browser.

Tickets

Track each show against the day card, then add booking references only to private notes or the ticket wallet.

  • Mark must-see events as Ticket needed until booked.
  • Keep QR codes and order numbers out of the public repo.
  • Use the print view for a clean travel briefing.

Getting Around

Waverley, tram stops, buses, walking time, and taxi pinch points can be added as the final group shape emerges.

  • Leave extra time near the Royal Mile and Old Town.
  • Put meet-up points on the map before busy days.
  • Keep accommodation addresses private.

Food

Use meal slots to avoid making every decision between shows. Add restaurant names once reservations are real.

  • Reserve group meals where timing matters.
  • Capture dietary notes privately.
  • Keep a low-effort fallback near each venue cluster.
