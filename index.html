Festival shortlist board

Build the Fringe week by slots.

Use this board to coordinate six daily planning slots from 10:00 to midnight, protect slot 5 for dinner, and prioritise theatre, comedic theatre, music, Traverse, EIF, and Fringe shows with strong review or producer signals.

Contributor brief

Sat-Thu should use six two-to-three-hour slots. The first Friday starts at 17:00 with three slots, including dinner.

Show selection

Prefer slot availability, multiple reputable five-star reviews from 2025 or 2024, reputable producers, and insider picks from outlets such as Time Out, Guardian, Times, or Scotsman.

Movement rule

Assume one hour for daytime performances, then allow 30 minutes or a specific walking-time check between shows.

Dinner base

Slot 5 is dinner. Look for Sat-Thu restaurants for eight people within a 20-minute walk of Edinburgh Playhouse / Greenside, avoiding Leith.

Sat-Thu full-day pattern

Use slots 1-6: 10-12, 12-14, 14-16, 16-18, dinner 18-21, and late 21-24. Keep 30 minutes of leeway between adjacent show slots.

First Friday pattern

Start from 17:00. Plan three slots only: an early arrival/show option, slot 5 dinner, and one late show or wildcard.

Wildcards

Allow two to four unusual or weird shows, but keep them labelled so they do not crowd out proven five-star candidates.

Six slot day shape

Slots 1-4 are show-first. Slot 5 is protected for dinner. Slot 6 is for a late show, music, or wildcard.

Selection criteria

Use these rules to decide what belongs in the main schedule.

  • 1Slot fit firstUse six slots Sat-Thu: 10-12, 12-14, 14-16, 16-18, dinner 18-21, and late 21-24. First Friday starts from 17:00 with three slots.
  • 2Review confidencePrefer multiple reputable five-star reviews from 2025 or 2024. Single reviews can be noisy, so mark them as weaker signals.
  • 3Producer and insider signalsPrioritise reputable producers such as Francesca Moody and informed picks from Time Out, Guardian, Times, Scotsman, and similar outlets.
  • 4Genre and programme biasBias theatre, comedic theatre, and music over stand-up and circus. Treat Fringe, Traverse Theatre, and EIF as priority programmes.
  • 5WildcardsAllow two to four weird wildcard shows, but label them clearly so they remain deliberate choices.
  • 6Dinner ruleSlot 5 is dinner. Book Sat-Thu tables for eight near Edinburgh Playhouse / Greenside, mixing about GBP100/head and GBP50/head options, with one longer-walk wildcard allowed and no Leith restaurants.
